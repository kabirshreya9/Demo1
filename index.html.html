About Our product

In this plastic age, literally all our packaging is being taken over by polyethylene and related plastics. The shortage of petroleum in the country and gulf crisis has reduced supplies of plastic granules. Hence reprocessed granules assume an important role in meeting the demand for plastic products. Lot of waste plastic from milk sachets, liquor covers, tobacco covers, packaging covers, broken buckets, tubs mugs etc. is available in large quantitites. Reprocessed plastic granules are used to produce packing covers, carry bags, buckets and mugs etc. Hence there is a good scope to recycle used plastic scrap to produce plastic granules, subject to guidelines laid down in Environment Act. .
